About Chain
and our services

Chain Coffee Bar started when a local coffee shop expedition became an eye openeing event into the world of a barista and latte art. The simple presentation of latte art in a cappuccino triggered an interest.

This interest started the learning process about coffee, latte art and eventually led to becoming a qualified barista. Chain is a way to experience the experience that triggered a love for coffee.

Coffee is what led to Chain and is the heart and soul of Chain. Specialty coffee is what we make and try to perfect in every cup. Great coffee is what we serve and great coffee is what we serve every time.

Chain Coffee Bar is a portable coffee bar that can be used at your event or function. Making it a quick, easy and simple solution to your coffee needs.
